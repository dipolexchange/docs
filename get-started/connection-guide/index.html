Connect Your Wallet to DiopleSwap

You've made a wallet and gotten your PRC20 tokens, now you just need to connect your wallet with DipoleSwap and you're good to go!

Check out the steps below for how to connect each of our recommended wallets to DipoleSwap.

  • ATON
  • MetaMask

To connect MetaMask to DipoleSwap follow the steps below.

Android and iOS‚Äã

  1. Open MetaMask and tap on the burger icon in the top-left.

  1. Tap Settings in the menu.

  1. In the Settings menu, tap on Network.

  1. Tap on the Add Network button at the bottom.

  1. On the page that opens, type in the following details:

Network Name: PlatON Chain

RPC Url: https://samurai.platon.network

Chain ID: 100

Symbol: LAT

Block Explorer URL: https://scan.platon.network

  1. Once you've confirmed entry of the new network, go back to the burger menu and tap Browser.

  1. Type "DipoleSwap" into the search field and start the search. The top result will be the DipoleSwap exchange. Tap to enter it.

  2. You wallet will ask you to connect. Tap Connect to connect to DipoleSwap.
